The Process

From first call to live website — in 5 steps

A clear, simple process so you always know exactly where we are.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a free 30-minute call to understand your business, your customers, and what you want your website to do. No jargon, no pressure — just a real conversation about what you need.

~30 minutes • Free • Video or phone
02

Design Proposal

We put together a design concept and page structure for your approval before we write a single line of code. You see exactly what you're getting — colours, layout, content — and we only move forward when you're happy.

2–3 business days • Revisions included
03

We Build It

Our team develops your website: clean code, mobile-first design, fast load times, and SEO-ready structure. We also write the copy so you don't have to stare at a blank page.

5–7 business days • Mobile & desktop • SEO included
04

Your Review

You get a live preview link to review the finished website. Want a different headline? Move a section? Change a colour? This is the stage to ask — all revisions are included.

Up to 2 rounds of revisions • Usually 1–2 days
05

Launch & Handover

We connect your domain, go live, and hand over full ownership. You get all the files, login credentials, and a short walkthrough so you can make small updates yourself whenever you want.

Same-day launch • Full ownership • You control everything
